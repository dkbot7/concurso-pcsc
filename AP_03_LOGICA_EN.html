1. PROPOSITIONAL LOGIC

What is a proposition?

A proposition is a statement that can be either TRUE or FALSE — only those two options. Commands and questions are not propositions.

Logical Connectives

ConnectiveSymbolTrue whenFalse when
AND (conjunction)∧Both sides are trueEither side is false
OR (disjunction)∨At least one side is trueBoth sides are false
NOT (negation)¬Original is falseOriginal is true
IF...THEN (conditional)→Always except when P=T and Q=FP is true AND Q is false
IF AND ONLY IF (biconditional)↔Both have the same valueValues differ
Most tested: The conditional (IF P THEN Q) is FALSE only when P is TRUE and Q is FALSE.
Its negation is: P AND NOT Q.

2. SYLLOGISMS

A syllogism has two premises and a conclusion. If the premises are true and the structure is valid, the conclusion must be true.

Premise 1: All police officers are public servants.
Premise 2: Maria is a police officer.
Conclusion: Therefore, Maria is a public servant. ✓
Tip: Validity depends on STRUCTURE, not on whether the content makes real-world sense.

3. PERCENTAGES

"Percent" = "per hundred." To find X% of a value: multiply value × (X ÷ 100).

OperationMethodExample
Find X% of NN × (X/100)20% of 500 = 500 × 0.20 = 100
Increase by X%N × (1 + X/100)+15% on 2,000 = 2,000 × 1.15 = 2,300
Discount of X%N × (1 - X/100)-20% on 300 = 300 × 0.80 = 240
Warning: +50% then -50% ≠ original value. Example: 100 → 150 → 75. Not 100!

PRACTICE QUESTIONS

Q1. The conditional "If P, then Q" is FALSE only when:

  • A) P is false and Q is true
  • B) P is true and Q is false
  • C) Both P and Q are false
  • D) Both P and Q are true
Answer: B — The conditional is false only when the hypothesis (P) is true and the conclusion (Q) is false.

Q2. A vehicle travels 180 km in 2 hours. What is its average speed?

  • A) 80 km/h
  • B) 85 km/h
  • C) 90 km/h
  • D) 95 km/h
Answer: C — S = D ÷ T = 180 ÷ 2 = 90 km/h.

Q3. What is the next number in the sequence: 3, 6, 12, 24, ___?

  • A) 36
  • B) 42
  • C) 48
  • D) 54
Answer: C — Geometric sequence with ratio ×2. 24 × 2 = 48.
