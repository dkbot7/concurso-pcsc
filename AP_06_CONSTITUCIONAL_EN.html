PRACTICE QUESTIONS

Q1. A police officer wants to search a residence. Without a warrant, entry is permitted ONLY in which situation?

  • A) If the officer suspects a crime was committed there
  • B) In case of flagrante delict (crime in progress) inside the dwelling
  • C) With the authorization of the police chief
  • D) During daytime hours without any additional requirement
Answer: B — The Constitution allows warrantless entry only for: flagrante delict, disaster/rescue, or daytime with resident's consent. Mere suspicion is not sufficient.

Q2. The Civil Police of Santa Catarina is responsible for:

  • A) Ostensive street policing and crime prevention
  • B) Investigating crimes and exercising the judicial police function
  • C) Guarding prisoners in the penitentiary system
  • D) Patrolling federal highways
Answer: B — Civil Police = judicial police function. Investigates crimes, leads police inquiries (inquérito policial).
