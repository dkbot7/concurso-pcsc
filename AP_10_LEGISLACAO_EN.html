1. CIVIL POLICE STRUCTURE — SC

The Civil Police of Santa Catarina (PCSC) is an agency of the State Executive Branch, linked to the Secretariat of Public Security. It exercises the judicial police function — investigating crimes and leading police inquiries.

2. DISCIPLINARY REGIME — DUTIES AND PROHIBITIONS

Main duties

  • Perform duties with dedication and care
  • Be loyal to the institutions
  • Observe all legal and regulatory norms
  • Treat people with courtesy and respect
  • Maintain confidentiality of information accessed through the position
  • Report to work at the designated time
  • Report any known irregularity to competent authority
  • Care for and conserve public property

Main prohibitions

  • Leave the workplace without authorization
  • Accept bribes, gifts, or any advantage related to the position
  • Use the position for personal benefit
  • Exercise another activity incompatible with the position
  • Disclose confidential information learned through the position
  • Give preferential treatment to relatives or friends

Interruption: Opening a sindicância or PAD interrupts the statute of limitations.

4. SINDICÂNCIA vs. PAD

FeatureSindicânciaPAD (Disciplinary Process)
When usedMinor infractionsSerious infractions — possible dismissal
Max punishmentSuspension up to 30 daysDismissal, retirement cancellation
Commission size1-2 members3 stable civil servants
Time limit30 days (extendable by 30)Longer — variable

PAD guarantees: Full right to adversarial proceedings and broad defense. Violation of these rights makes the PAD null and void.

5. POSITION ACCUMULATION

The Constitution generally prohibits accumulating public positions. Exceptions for civil police officers:

  • One teaching position
  • One health sector position (with schedule compatibility)
Accumulating positions outside these exceptions is a serious infraction and may result in dismissal.

PRACTICE QUESTIONS

Q1. An officer commits an infraction punishable by warning (advertência). After how long does the right to impose this punishment expire?

  • A) 5 years
  • B) 2 years
  • C) 180 days
  • D) 90 days
Answer: C — 180 days for warning, 2 years for suspension, 5 years for dismissal. Memorize this table!

Q2. A civil police officer is suspected of an infraction that could result in dismissal. The correct procedure to investigate is:

  • A) Sindicância — sufficient for any infraction
  • B) PAD — mandatory when dismissal is a possible outcome
  • C) Simple administrative hearing — no formal process needed
  • D) The police chief decides informally
Answer: B — When the infraction may result in dismissal (or other severe punishments), the PAD is mandatory, with full constitutional guarantees.

Q3. Which of the following is a lawful accumulation of positions for a civil police officer?

  • A) Police officer + civil engineer at a state company
  • B) Police officer + teacher at a public university
  • C) Police officer + administrative officer at another state agency
  • D) Police officer + military police officer
Answer: B — Teaching is one of the two allowed accumulations. The other is one health-sector position with schedule compatibility.
